About Me


 Hello, world! I'm Seonaid: a Full Stack Developer, Teacher, Writer, and Forever-Curious Person. Here is my attempt at brevity:

After a decade of work in the academic and health sectors (and several thousand moments of reflection), I decided it was time to explore my interest in software development in the summer of 2021. I wrote my first line of JavaScript in August, checked all of the logic books out of my local library soon thereafter, and enrolled in CodeOp's Full Stack Development bootcamp in September. I've built several full-stack web applications since then, and now regularly dream in curly brackets.

Currently, I am working on maintaining my React, JS, and MySQL knowledge, while studying algorithms, data structures, and Python as I search for my first role as a junior developer. Outside of tech, I'm a big fan of dance, literature, art, and laughing at almost anything. I'll be using this site to showcase my work, document my learning, and hopefully to inspire others who may be considering learning new technical skills. Don't hesitate to get in touch on LinkedIn if you have any questions!
